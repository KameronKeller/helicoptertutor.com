Free Digital Logbook for Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers, and Open Office

  • Sortable columns (sort function does not work in Numbers version)
  • Automatic IACRA/FAA 8710 output
  • Pilot currency check, calculates number of days remaining for flight reviews, SFAR flight reviews, CFI expiration, medical certificate, and solo endorsements.
  • Extra columns, fully editable

Google Sheets is recommended for automatic cloud sync, and ability to use from any computer with internet.
